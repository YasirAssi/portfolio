Tic-Tac-Toe, also known as X and O, is a classic two-player game played on a 3x3 grid. The game involves two players, usually represented by "X" and "O," taking turns to mark empty cells in the grid. The goal is to form a line of three of your symbols (either "X" or "O") horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
